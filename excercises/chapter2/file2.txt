Virat Kohli, the former captain of the Indian cricket team, is a right-handed top-order batsman renowned for his consistency and aggressive playing style. 
He is widely considered one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport, holding numerous records in international cricket. 
Known as 'King Kohli', he has scored over 26,000 runs across all three formats of the game. 
His fitness regimen and work ethic are often cited as benchmarks for aspiring athletes. 
Off the field, he is involved in various philanthropic initiatives and is a prominent global sports icon.
